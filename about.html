About

Umang is the annual inter-college sports festival hosted by the International Institute of Information Technology Bangalore (IIITB). It is a thrilling celebration of athleticism, competition, and sportsmanship, attracting participants from various colleges and universities . Every year, Umang welcomes over 1000 participants who clash in an epic showdown across a multitude of sports, from slam-dunking on the basketball court to spiking it in volleyball. It's an electrifying display of talent you won't want to miss. Umang isn't just about winning, it's about the journey. Whether you're a participant or a supporter, these exhilarating days will craft memories. Gear up for a thrilling and frolicsome experience like no other! Don't miss your chance to be part of the Umang legacy.at will stay with you forever.
